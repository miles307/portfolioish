About Miles

Miles is an Electrical Engineering student at Cal Poly SLO. He is interested in Aerospace, Nuclear and sustainable energy, and sustainable transportation, driven by a desire to contribute to innovative solutions in these fields. Miles possesses a unique blend of outdoor leadership and critical problem-solving skills honed through extensive backcountry skiing and dedicated volunteer service with Teton County Search and Rescue, experiences cultivated during his time with Boy Scouts of America. Miles also competes in Mountain and road cycling at the collegiate level as a valued member of the Cal Poly Club Cycling Team, demonstrating his commitment and discipline.
